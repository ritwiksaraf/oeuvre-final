The Oeuvre Team is the upcoming trophy in the showcase that Fr. C. Rodriguez Institute of Technology holds in its arsenal of innovative and progressive apprentice. The team showcases interdepartmental cohesion, comprehensive surge in dexterity of every member and most importantly the resolve to divulge the creative faces hidden inside the engineering students.

The Oeuvre team is based on the fundamental values of agnels such as integrity, equality and discipline. We at oeuvre work our hardest so that the students at Fr. C. Rodriguez have a portal to release their share of creativity to make this course more fulfilling. The team is a blend of students working over moderation, Public Relations and Web development and design which has managed to host the first of its kind College blog under the Mumbai university and continues to grow as the platform for engineers to share ideas and read about the various endevors of their peers.
